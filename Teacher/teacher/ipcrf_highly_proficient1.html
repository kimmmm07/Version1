Classroom Observable Objective/Indicator (COI) Rating:

To get the rating for Quality, encode COT rating per observation period by doing the following steps:

  1. Select the subject for COT.
  2. Input the COT rating per objective.
  3. Input the date of observation.

Once all available COT ratings are inputted, encode your rating for Efficiency.

Note: This form automatically transmits the COT rating to its corresponding RPMS 5-point scale.
