Results-Based Performance Management System (RPMS)

Instructions Teacher work

KRA 3: Curriculum and Planning

21 points

7% | Objective 9 (Set achievable and appropriate learning outcomes that are aligned with learning competencies.)

  • Means of Verification (MOV)
  • At least one (1) lesson plan (e.g., DLP, DLL, WHLP, WLP, WLL, Lesson Exemplars, and the likes) or one lesson from a self-learning module, developed by the ratee* and used in instruction, with achievable and appropriate learning outcomes that are aligned with the learning competencies as shown in any one (1) of the following:
    1. lecture/discussion
    2. activity/activity sheet
    3. performance task
    rubric for assessing performance using criteria that appropriately describe the target output

7% | Objective 10 (Used strategies for providing timely, accurate and constructive feedback to improve learner performance.)

  • Means of Verification (MOV)
  • Classroom Observation Tool (COT) rating sheet/s or inter-observer agreement form/s

7% | Objective 11 (Utilized assessment data to inform the modification of teaching and learning practices and programs.)

  • Means of Verification (MOV)
  • A list of identified least/most mastered skills based on the frequency of errors/correct responses with any one (1) of the following supporting MOVs:
    1. accomplishment report for remedial/enhancement activities (e.g., remedial sessions, Summer Reading Camp, Phil-IRI-based reading program)
    2. intervention material used for remediation/reinforcement/enhancement
    3. lesson plan/activity log for remediation/enhancement utilizing assessment data to modify teaching and learning practices or programs
