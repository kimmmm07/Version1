Instructions Teacher work

KRA 4: Curriculum and Planning & Assessment and Reporting

21 points

7% | Objective 12 (Established safe and secure learning environments to enhance learning through the consistent implementation of policies, guidelines and procedures.)

  • Means of Verification (MOV)
  • Any one (1) of the following:
    1. Proof of participation in any activity highlighting the objective, such as, but not limited the following:
    - Receipt form/monitoring form during distribution of learning materials, etc.
    - Commitment form to stakeholders, developed advocacy materials, certificate of participation that shows parents’/stakeholders’ engagement signed by the school head, etc.
    - Home visitation forms
    - Any equivalent ALS form/document that highlights the objective
    - Others (please specify and provide annotations)
    2. Parent-teacher log or proof of other stakeholders meeting (e.g., one-on-one parent-teacher learner conference log; attendance sheet with minutes of online or face-to-face meeting; proof of involvement in the learners’/parents’ orientation, etc.)
    3. Any form of communication to parents/stakeholders (e.g., notice of meeting; screenshot of chat/text message/communication with parent/guardian)

7% | Objective 13 (Participated in professional networks to share knowledge and to enhance practice)

  • Means of Verification (MOV)
  • 1. Certificate of completion in a course/training
    2. Certificate of participation in a webinar, retooling, upskilling, and other training/seminar/workshop with proof of implementation
    3. Certificate of recognition/ speakership in a webinar and other training/seminar/workshop
    4. Any proof of participation in a benchmarking activity
    5. Any proof of participation in school LAC sessions (online/face-to-face) certified by the LAC Coordinator
    6. Others (please specify and provide annotations)

7% | Objective 14 (Developed a personal improvement plan based on reflection of one’s practice and ongoing professional learning)

  • Means of Verification (MOV)
  • 1. Certification from the ICT Coordinator / School Head / Focal Person in charge of e-SAT
    2. IPCRF-DP
    3. Mid-year Review Form (MRF) Updated IPCRF-DP from Phase II
