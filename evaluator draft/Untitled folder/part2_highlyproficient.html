CORE BEHAVIORAL COMPETENCIES
Self-Management 1. Sets personal goals and direction, needs and development.
2. Undertakes personal actions and behavior that are clear and purposeful.
3. Displays emotional maturity and enthusiasm.
4. Prioritizes work tasks and schedules effectively.
5. Sets challenging, realistic goals for self and others. 		3
Teamwork 1. Willingly accepts responsibility.
2. Promotes collaboration and removes barriers.
3. Applies negotiation principles.
4. Drives consensus.
5. Works constructively with others. 		4
Professionalism and Ethics 1. Demonstrates values and behavior aligned with policies.
2. Practices ethical and professional behavior.
3. Maintains professional image and punctuality.
4. Sacrifices personal needs to meet organizational goals.
5. Helps others improve their effectiveness. 		3
Service Orientation 1. Explains and articulates directions, issues, and problems.
2. Takes responsibility for dealing with and solving customer issues.
3. Promotes advocacy.
4. Develops and adopts service improvement programs.
5. Improves service delivery. 		3
Results Focus 1. Achieves results with optimal use of time and resources.
2. Avoids rework, mistakes, and wastage.
3. Delivers error-free outputs.
4. Expresses a desire to do better and eliminate inefficiency.
5. Makes changes in work methods to improve performance. 		3
Innovation 1. Examines the root cause of problems.
2. Demonstrates an ability to think outside the box.
3. Promotes creative climate.
4. Translates creative thinking into tangible changes.
5. Uses ingenious methods to achieve responsibilities. 		3
Note: The assessment in the demonstration of competencies shall not be reflected in the final rating. These competencies are monitored to inform professional development plans.
